New screen added
Reminder settings were buried in a generic Settings page. Given how critical notifications are for this app, they deserve their own dedicated screen.
Quiet hours
Quiet hours with a critical medicine override is an important edge case. A 2 AM insulin alarm is very different from a vitamin reminder — the app should know the difference.

Unlimited snoozing defeats the purpose. A hard cap of 3 snoozes then escalates to "missed" — honest tracking and caregiver alerts still trigger correctly.
